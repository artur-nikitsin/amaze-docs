Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Amaze! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile game application.

Information We Collect

Amaze is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Game Progress: Your game progress, scores, and collected items are stored locally on your device.
  • In-App Purchases: Purchase information is processed through Apple's App Store. We do not store your payment information.
  • Game Center: If you use Game Center, your achievements and leaderboard data are managed by Apple.

Third-Party Services

Amaze uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob (for free version)

  • Used to display advertisements in the free version of the game
  • May collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

Apple StoreKit

  • Used to process in-app purchases (Premium version)
  • Managed entirely by Apple
  • Privacy Policy: Apple Privacy Policy

Apple Game Center

  • Used for achievements and leaderboards (optional)
  • Managed entirely by Apple

Data Storage

  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not have access to your game data
  • Data is not transmitted to our servers
  • Deleting the app removes all stored data

Premium Version

When you purchase the Premium version:

  • All advertisements are removed
  • No ad-related data collection occurs
  • Purchase is processed securely through Apple's App Store

Children's Privacy

Amaze is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data collection is handled by Apple's systems with appropriate protections in place.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by deleting the app)
  • Opt out of personalized ads (through iOS Settings)
  • Disable ad tracking (iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
