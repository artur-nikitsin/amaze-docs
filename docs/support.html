Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I play Amaze?

Swipe in any direction (up, down, left, right) to move your character. The maze scrolls automatically from right to left. Collect cheese, avoid going off the left edge, and use pickaxes to break walls when needed.

Q: What happens when I lose all lives?

When you lose all three lives, the game ends. You can start a new game and try to beat your high score!

Q: How do pickaxes work?

Pickaxes let you destroy the wall to your right. You start with 3 pickaxes and can collect more in the maze. The pickaxe button is only active when there's a wall next to you.

Q: What is the Story Mode?

As you play, you can collect letters scattered throughout the maze. These letters gradually reveal hidden stories about your character's adventures. Check the Stories screen to see your progress!

Q: What does Premium version include?

Premium version removes all advertisements and doubles your starting resources (6 lives and 6 pickaxes instead of 3). It's a one-time purchase that works forever!

Q: How do I restore my Premium purchase?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Premium section ‚Üí tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore your purchase on any device using the same Apple ID.

Q: Can I play without ads?

Yes! Purchase the Premium version to remove all advertisements permanently.

Q: How do I turn off sound/music?

Tap the Settings icon (gear) in the game, then use the toggles to control Background Music, Sound Effects, and Vibration.

Q: Is my game progress saved?

Yes! Your high scores, collected stories, and settings are automatically saved on your device.

Q: What are the system requirements?

Amaze requires iOS 17.0 or later. Compatible with iPhone XS and newer models.

Troubleshooting

Issue: Game runs slowly or lags
  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest version of the game
Issue: Premium purchase not working
  • Check your internet connection
  • Sign out and back into the App Store
  • Try "Restore Purchases" in Settings
  • Contact us if the problem persists
Issue: No sound/music
  • Check your device volume
  • Make sure Silent mode is off
  • Check in-game Settings to ensure audio is enabled
